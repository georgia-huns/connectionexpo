About the Expo

From "Where do I start?" to "When do I start?"

The Connection Expo is the first event of its kind in Guelph. A free afternoon where you can meet the people behind the city's clubs, organizations, and volunteer groups, face to face and all in one place.

By the time you leave you'll know exactly what exists in this city, who's running it, and how to get involved. No Googling, no guessing, just real conversations with real people who want you there.

Every organization at the Expo was selected because they're building something that helps people connect, grow, and feel like they belong.

Hosted by Friends of Friends. Open to everyone.
