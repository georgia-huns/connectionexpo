About the Expo

Walk in curious. Walk out connected.

The Connection Expo is a free afternoon where you'll meet the people behind Guelph's clubs, organizations, and volunteer groups — face to face, in one place.

By the time you leave, you'll know exactly what exists in this city, who's running it, and how to get involved. No Googling, no guessing — just real conversations with real people who want you there.

Hosted by Friends of Friends. Open to everyone.

Event Details

Saturday, September 20, 2026
12:00 PM – 4:00 PM
Guelph Farmers' Market
2 Gordon St, Guelph, ON
Free admission — no tickets required
Accessible venue — wheelchair accessible entrances at the side of the building on Gordon St and at the back from the parking lot. Gender-neutral bathrooms available.
